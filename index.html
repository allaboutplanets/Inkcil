Inkcil

OUR AIM

Enviroment Friendly

We intend to eliminate significant amounts of plastic produced by plastic pens in our classrooms and offices.

Health Benefits

There is mounting evidence that many types of plastic can have a direct harmful impact on children's health.

Pricing?

It is expected that the retail price of inkcil will be comparable to exsiting writing implements

Easily Customisable

Inkcil would be easily customisable with different colours, ink and branding.

Did You Know?

The world now produces more than 380 million tonnes of plastic every year, which could end up as pollutants, entering our natural environment and oceans.

100% Recyclable

Inkcil will be made up of 100% recyclable elements.

What I require assistance with.

After some technical imput from material scientists I intend approaching current pen and pencil manufacturers to explore the possibility of adapting existing machinery to be able to fabricate my plastic free pen INKCIL. I also need support in protecting my concept and having it registered. Further in the future I envisage lobbying government to remove harmful plastics from the classroom completely. As a student it is difficult to access the necessary agencies who can help me to achieve this.

Why Inkcil?

What is the competitive advantage of Inkcil? Why will people choose inkcil above other brands?

I have made a working prototype of the pen which is 100% plastic free. My initial customer base will be students but it can expand to all people who use a pen- a very big customer base. The advantages to the customer include being more responsible in the protection of our environment and the freedom from worry about the toxicity of plastics.

Where do I intent to implement Inkcil by the end of 2022?

I intend to implement the idea in classrooms in my own school and city if i can get the support, previously mentioned, to finalise the materials to be used in manufacturing the product and can secure funding to get an initial batch of the INKCILS made.
